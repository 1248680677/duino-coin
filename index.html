About Duino-Coin

There are many cryptocurrencies available. Most of them can be mined, but only using powerful CPUs, GPUs or ASICs.
I've wanted to do something related to cryptocurrencies with Arduino because nobody has done that yet. That's why Duino-Coin was created.
A cryptocurrency that can be mined with regular home computers, smartphones, Arduino boards and many other platforms.

 

Duino-Coin is:

• available worldwide - all you need is internet access

• cost effective - explained in this blog post

• open-source - anyone can contribute - Duino-Coin GitHub

• centralized - no need to download gigabytes of stuff, you won't lose your coins

 

Why should you mine it?

• DUCO (Duino-Coin) is worth ~0.1 XMG (Coin Magi), exchange here

• mining Duino-Coins isn't very CPU intensive

• it can be mined using various platforms

• mining on regular computers, smartphones is profitable

 

Why would you want to mine on Arduino?

• you may have some Arduino boards that don't do anything

• watching Arduino do something that produces money is quite satisfying

• because now you can!
