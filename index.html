 Why should you use Duino-Coin

  1. It's the only coin that can be mined on Arduino Boards, Smartphones, Computers and Single-Board Computers
  2. It does many things differently than other cryptos
  3. It's a completely original creation (isn't just a lazy fork)
  4. It has instant transactions (you don't have to wait half an hour to transfer funds)
  5. It's completely open source and in constant development for 2 years already
  6. It wants to achieve something new in the crypto world
  7. It's price may go up in the future

Why not give it a try when it's all free, you will learn something new and make money in the process?


 Technical specifications

  •  Ticker: DUCO (ᕲ)
  •  Coin supply: Infinite (before December 2020: 350k coins)
  •  Premine: < 5k blocks (< 500coins) from early development
  •  Transaction time: Instant
  •  Decimals: 20
  •  Price fom DUCO Exchange: 1 ᕲ = $0.0096
  •  Algorithms: DUCO-S1 (+DUCO-S1A) with more planned, including PoS
  •  Rewards: supported by original Kolka system helping reward miners fairly
  •  Accounts registered on network: 1000

 What is the point of mining on AVR boards

Simply saying - because now you can. There is literally NO other cryptocurrency that supports it.
Many people have Arduinos that are just laying around waiting for better days.
We thought it'd be a cool idea to put these small devices in use in the crypto world.
We wanted to create something new, innovative and unique, and from the size of community we've gathered, it looks like we made ultra low-energy devices mining possible.

If the idea of setting it all up sounds hard for you - don't worry. We have some nice guides to make it all easy.
